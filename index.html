About

Why work with me?

I am a highly skilled data analyst with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights that drive business growth. With a solid foundation in data analysis, statistical modeling, and data visualization, I have the expertise to address complex problems and deliver meaningful solutions.

I am proficient in a wide range of data analytics tools, including SQL, Shiny, IBM SPSS, Stata, R Studio, Python, PowerBI, Eviews, Gretl, Jamovi, Tableau, NVivo, Splunk, and Excel. My deep understanding of these tools allows me to effectively manipulate and analyze large datasets, tailoring my approach to the specific needs of each project.

My experience spans across various industries, including social sciences, marketing, healthcare, finance, retail, and technology. This diverse background has equipped me with the ability to tackle complex problems and deliver tailored solutions.
